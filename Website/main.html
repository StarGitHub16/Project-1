Information About Us:


Our mission is to strive for an environment all fellow gamers can enjoy! At our venue we come prepared with full equipment such as various controllers, TVs, consoles and arcade booths! If you are looking to host weekly or even monthly tournaments, this is the place for you!


Cost of entry is $10, hosting a reservation is $5, Tournament cost entry (if any) will be up to the Organizers themselves!


If you are wanting to reserve the whole venue for anything major or just to make sure you will make it in, head on over to our Sign Up page now!
